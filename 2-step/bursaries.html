SECTION 8B: DISCOUNT APPLICATIONS

Discounts are applicable on the fee (excluding the deposit) for first time registrations only. In order to qualify for the discount the completed registration contract must be submitted and the deposit must be paid in full. The discounted amount is based on the month the deposit is paid, as follows:

Before July 2017 - 7%,
August 2017 - 6%,
September 2017 - 5%,
October 2017 - 4%,
November 2017 - 3%. (Only for January 2018 Intake)

Please note that if any subsequent Vega campaign equates to a lower fee for the equivalent 2018 registration, you will qualify for the lower fee upon request
