New Registration

Welcome to Vega Online Registration. Please follow the steps below:

Step 1
  1. Complete your qualification and module details.
  2. Complete your personal information.
  3. Complete account payer details.
  4. Complete your emergency/additional contact details.
  5. If you qualify for a discount, please refer to Annexure C
  6. Upload all original certified documents.
  7. Accept the terms and conditions.
  8. Submit Part 1 of your online registration contract.
Step 2
  1. One of our Navigators will contact you to confirm your payment method and related details.
  2. Confirm your registration and payment details already captured.
  3. Submit Part 2 of your online registration contract.
  4. Proceed to pay your fees.

Should you have any questions regarding your online registration please contact your campus directly.
