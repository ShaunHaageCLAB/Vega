Select an option below to begin your registration process, or to continue with your existing application.

New Students

To ensure your online integrity, please enter your email address and Application ID below. If you are not sure of your Application ID – please contact campus. If you have not yet received an Application ID – you may enter your ID number in order to proceed – however for security purposes your online profile will be updated with your Application ID at a later stage.

Returning Students

To ensure your online integrity and to ensure that the correct information populates, you MUST enter your student number and VCConnect email address below in order to proceed. Please note that as a returning student you are not required to provide us with your personal details again. We have populated this data where applicable - please check that all of your information is correct– if any of your details have changed since your last registration, please ensure that these details are amended during this registration process. You must ensure however that you use your student number to register or your information will not populate.
